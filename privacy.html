1. Collection of Personal Data

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users through Hardy Ninja.

Hardy Ninja integrates Google Play Games services to allow users to unlock achievements and access leaderboards. Google Play Games may collect data in accordance with its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy for more information.

3. Data Storage

Player progress is stored locally on the device in a file. We do not send any data related to gameplay or actions to external servers.

4. Required Permissions

To function properly, Hardy Ninja requires the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: This permission is necessary to save player progress on the device.
  • Internet Access: This permission is required to connect to Google Play Games services and display ads from third-party advertising partners.

5. Advertisements

Hardy Ninja may display advertisements provided by third parties. These services may use information such as advertising identifiers to personalize ads. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored directly by us.

6. Minimum Age

Hardy Ninja is intended for users aged 13 and above. If you are under 13 years old, please do not use this game.

7. Security

We do not store any personal data on external servers. In-game progress data is stored locally on the device, minimizing the risk of leaks or breaches.
